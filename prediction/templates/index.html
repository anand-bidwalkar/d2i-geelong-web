About us

This Project is about Creating an integrated system which involve the designing and development of a web-based data analytics dashboard for the city of Greater Geelong Council. This system is designed to provide the insights to council for solving challenges involved in the development of City

The final product will contain the functions like, Predicting the population, Comparison of the population, predicting new train stations, predicting new timetable of trains from a station, Detailed Suburb information, Predicts the Accident-prone areas, Predicts the foot count, and finally the Dashboard which displays various visualizations of these predictions.

We are a team of 11 students which is named after D2i Geelong city Squad are working on this project as a part of Capstone Unit. We are working under the administration of our unit chair, mentors, Client and DISC.

Geelong City

Geelong is a port city located on Corio Bay and the Barwon River, in the state of Victoria, Australia. Geelong is 75 kilometres (47 mi) south-west of the state capital, Melbourne. It is the second largest Victorian city, with an estimated urban population of 268,277 as of June 2018.
